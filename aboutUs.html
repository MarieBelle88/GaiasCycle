About Us

Our Mission

At Gaia's Cycle, our mission is to revolutionize urban agriculture by employing cutting-edge green technologies and resource-efficient methods. We strive to supply local communities with sustainable, healthy, and locally produced food, directly addressing environmental challenges and enhancing food security.

Our Vision

Gaia's Cycle envisions a world where urban farming meets societal needs sustainably, reducing CO₂ emissions in the food sector while contributing to global climate goals. Our work aligns with the United Nations’ Sustainable Development Goals, the 2030 Agenda, and Germany's climate neutrality goal for 2045.

Sustainable Impact Summary

Gaia’s Cycle has developed eco-friendly farming methods that contribute to significant CO₂ reductions. By using renewable energy and optimizing resources, we have saved approximately 450 metric tons of CO₂ emissions in the past year compared to traditional farming methods.

Our farming systems are designed to be self-sufficient, powered by solar energy, heat pumps, and ice storage, contributing to a 40% decrease in water usage and an 80% reduction in land usage. Our model is proving that urban farming can be both productive and sustainable.

Our Journey in Sustainable Urban Farming

Since our inception, Gaia's Cycle has pioneered sustainable practices in urban farming. From our first 100m² prototype to our current state-of-the-art 1,000m² facility, we've scaled up, innovated, and contributed to climate goals. Each milestone in our history reinforces our commitment to urban agriculture and sustainability.

Gaia's Cycle is committed to transforming urban agriculture for a sustainable, resilient food system.
